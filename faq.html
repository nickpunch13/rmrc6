Frequently Asked Questions


Q. What conditions can I treat with the services of the Remedial massage and Rehabilitation Center?


A. We treat a wide range of conditions which include but are not limited to the following:

  • Acute and chronic neck and back pain (including disc injuries)
  • Muscle tears
  • Skeletal deformities (scoliosis, kyphosis)
  • Neurological pain
  • Sciatica
  • Headaches and migraines
  • Stress related problems
  • Tiredness
  • Pains in peripheral joints such as knees, ankles, elbows and shoulders
  • Arthritic pain
  • Sports related injuries and recovery after any skeletal trauma
  • Work related and repetitive strain injuries
  • Asthma
  • Contractures of joints
  • High blood pressure (young people)
  • Vision improvement
  • Cerebral palsy
  • Weight loss and lots more.

Q. Who will be providing my treatment?


A. You will be assessed and treated by 2 highly qualified medical doctors with over 20 years of practical experience in orthopaedic, trauma and neurosurgery, obstetrics and gynaecology, general therapy and neurology, osteopathy and manual therapy, and different types of massage. Our team delivers high quality treatment to clients of all ages.

Q. What do I need to do before my first visit?

A. Please, read all the information on our site. If you have any questions, contact us by the phone or email. You can book your visit through our online booking system (preferred) or through the phone/email. Also, you can download the health history form from our site, fill it in and bring it with you to our office on the day of your appointment. It will save you more time during your assessment and treatment. The form should be signed in our office.


Q. What will happen during my first visit?

A. You should arrive to the office 5-10 minutes before your scheduled time and fill in the health history form, if you have not done so at home. After the assessment, we will explain our findings to you and develop your personal treatment plan according to your wishes and goals.

Q. How can I make my payment?

A. At this moment, we only accept cash and checks.

Q. Is it possible to get a therapist to visit me at home?

A. No problems! We can organise a mobile therapy session for you! Give us a call to find out more!

Q. What is the difference between Post-Isometric Myorelaxation (PIR) and Remedial Massage?

A. Our body is a very complicated organism. Remedial and other types of massage can help you to improve its condition. The purpose of PIR is to remove the cause of your problem completely and help you build your muscle frame to prevent troubles in the future. A human body should be treated as a whole, from head to toe. We use a combination of PIR and other types of massage, suitable for specific conditions, to treat your medical problems during each visit treating not one but many problems at the same time.
