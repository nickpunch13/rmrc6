Why Post-Isometric Myorelaxation and Remedial Massage?


The post-isometric relaxation technique begins by placing the muscle in a stretched position. Then an isometric contraction is exerted against some level of resistance. Relaxation and then gentle stretch follow as the muscle releases. This technique was applied to tight, tender muscles that are commonly associated with musculoskeletal pain. With a combination of remedial and relaxation massage elements we can stretch all the muscles of your body and will find the point of balance of your health.
